What We Do

Underwriting and Advisory Services for Real Estate Acquisitions

From first look to closing, Omri Investments supports acquisition, recapitalization, and development decisions with rigorous, institutional-caliber analysis.

01

Acquisition Underwriting & Financial Modeling

Custom Excel models — IRR, discounted cash flow, and equity waterfalls — built for office, multifamily, retail, and hospitality acquisitions.

02

Financial & Transaction Due Diligence

Deal-level analysis of debt, equity, and payback periods so you know exactly what you're underwriting before you commit capital.

03

Capital Structure Advisory

Structuring the right mix of senior debt, mezzanine, and equity — including TIC and DST structures — to fit a deal's risk and return profile.

04

Market & Feasibility Analysis

Feasibility studies that stress-test acquisition, recapitalization, and development scenarios before you go to contract.

05

Investor Reporting & Communications

Quarterly investor updates and distribution waterfall reporting that keeps LPs and partners confident and informed.

06

Deal Structuring & Negotiation Support

Hands-on support through negotiation and contract strategy, from LOI to closing table.
